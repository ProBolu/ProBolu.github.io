Intro

I am a BSc (Hons) Game Development graduate from Staffordshire University, with a strong foundation in software development and a passion for creating interactive experiences. Over the years, I’ve built a diverse set of skills through developing both 2D and 3D games from the ground up, designing visual effects, and working on Mixed Reality projects. You can explore some of my personal and academic work on the Projects page.

What sets me apart is my commitment to continuous learning, adaptability, and a genuine passion for problem-solving. I take pride in staying up to date with the latest technologies and tools, allowing me to quickly adapt to new workflows and contribute effectively to any development team. My ability to learn fast and stay motivated enables me to meet challenges head-on and grow with the evolving tech.

I am currently seeking a full-time role where I can apply my skills and experiences in a meaningful way. My goal is to be part of a collaborative and agile environment where I can contribute, learn, and make an impact. You can read more about my background and the steps I’ve taken to pursue a career in the software and games industry on the Journey page.

    • Journey

    I have always had a keen interest in Software Engineering, which is why I have prioritised pursuing opportunities that can help me build a successful career in the industry. This includes carefully selecting the courses and subjects I studied in secondary school and sixth form, as well as participating in relevant experiences and opportunities.

    Before securing a Distinction/Merit in my Game Development course at sixth form, where I learned various art-related techniques used in the industry alongside coding skills (primarily block-based programming and some C++ in Unreal Engine 4), I first earned a CIDA certificate and a Tech Award in Creative Media Production. In the process of achieving these qualifications, I gained substantial knowledge that I believe will support my journey to becoming a skilled software programmer. Prior to and during my pursuit of a BSc in Games Programming, I further expanded my knowledge and experience as a web developer at a company. I was responsible for creating and managing the functionalities of the main website owned by HighZone Care Recruitment Agency. I have recently graduated with a BSc (Hons) in Game Development from Staffordshire University, where I gained a strong foundation in programming, game engines, and software design.

    Alongside my final year at university, I challenged myself by taking part in several external courses and industry-led virtual experiences. Through platforms such as Forage, Skyscanner, and EA, I completed projects including:

    • Using React Backpack to explore front-end development practices at Skyscanner.
    • Designing C++ classes and proposing a game concept as part of EA’s virtual experience.
    • Creating a scalable cloud hosting framework using AWS Elastic Beanstalk as part of the AWS Early Careers Program.

    I also completed an intensive 8-week Microsoft Power Up course, where I learned how to build applications and automations using Microsoft Power Apps and Power Automate. Upon completion, I earned a digital badge to certify my achievement.

    In the final months of university, I was hired as a Power Platform Developer on a six-month contract at Horizonwork Partners Limited. During my time there, I:

    • Designed and developed custom business applications using Power Apps and Power Automate.
    • Built responsive user interfaces with low-code programming.
    • Created automated workflows to improve business efficiency using Power Automate.
    • Integrated solutions with Microsoft Dataverse for secure data management.
    • Developed Power BI dashboards to visualize company performance, which were presented in planning and decision-making meetings.

    These experiences have not only strengthened my technical capabilities but also helped me grow as a developer who’s adaptable, eager to learn, and ready to contribute meaningfully in both the software and games industry.
